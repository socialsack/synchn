Industry Meet

SYNCHN 2025

SYNCHN 2025 is the premier annual biotech industry meet, bringing together leading scientists, industry experts, startups, and policymakers to foster collaboration and innovation. The event features a dynamic program of seminars, panel discussions, and interactive workshops designed to accelerate translational research and strengthen the biotechnology ecosystem in India.

Highlights

SYNCHN 2025 - Biotech Industry Meet

Key Highlights of This Year's Event Innovation Collaboration Translation Impact

Focus on Collaboration

SYNCHN 2025 reinforces the critical need for sustained partnerships between academic institutions and industry players to drive innovation in biotechnology.

Synergy in Science

The event serves as a platform to build scientific synergy through collaborative research, resulting in high-quality data, cutting-edge technologies, and translational outcomes.

Spotlight on the NCR Biotech Cluster

Hosting the event at THSTI’s state-of-the-art campus underscores the strategic importance of the NCR Biotech Cluster as a hub for biotech innovation and entrepreneurship.

Industry Engagement

With participation from startups, established companies, and policy makers, SYNCHN 2025 encourages open dialogue on current challenges, future opportunities, and evolving expectations from academia.

THSTI’s Role

As a premier institute under BRIC, THSTI plays a pivotal role in translating biomedical research into real-world healthcare solutions. SYNCHN serves as a gateway for industry partners to engage directly with THSTI’s investigators and emerging scientific talent.
